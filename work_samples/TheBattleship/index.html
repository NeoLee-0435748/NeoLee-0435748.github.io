Directions:

You have only 30 turns to attempt to sink all five ships. During each turn, you can pick one of a map coordinate representing the location at which you wish to fire a missile. After each missile shot attempt, your program will know whether the chosen coordinate is a hit or a miss. The targeting map will be updated to show the latest missile result and be shown to you. A message indicating the current missile count will also be displayed, used to tell you how many turns remain.

If a picked coordinate value is picked, your pick is invalid until a valid coordinate is picked.

The game has two ending conditions:

 • If you hits every individual location in the map that contains part of a ship before running out of missiles, you win the game.

 • If you runs out of missiles before hitting every part of every ship, you lose the game.
